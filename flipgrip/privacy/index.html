PRIVACY POLICY

I, the developer, have NO access to any information that may personally identify anyone who plays Flip Grip or visits this website. I do, however, integrate some third-party software, which in turn may collect some data from you. Although this practice is quite commonplace, you have the right to know exactly what information is obtained, the reasons why that information is collected and where you can learn more about that software.

Flip Grip uses a third-party service called “Unity Analytics” to obtain data about general gameplay events, such as when you win or lose a level or reach a certain amount of game points. This data is collected in an anonymous, aggregated form, making it impossible to identify any particular players from it. I can use the data, for example, to make adjustments to the levels that are statistically more difficult to complete. You can read the privacy policy of “Unity Analytics” here: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

Periodically, Flip Grip may give you the opportunity to watch an advertisement in exchange for game points. This allows you to unlock new levels more quickly, and helps me make some money. This feature is unobtrusive and can be completely ignored. The advertisements are served by a third-party network called “Unity Ads”, which, for its own purposes, may collect some basic information. You can read the privacy policy of “Unity Ads” here: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

This website uses a third-party service called “Google Analytics”, which in turn uses cookies. It allows me to know things like the approximate number of people who have visited this website and what countries they are predominantly from. All collected information is anonymous and can’t be used to personally identify you. You can read the privacy policy of “Google Analytics” here: https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or any other issues, please send an email to support@danijmn.com and I will be glad to help you out.
