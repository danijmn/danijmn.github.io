Flip Grip
Developer: Daniel Nora
Platforms: iPhone® and iPad®
Release date: April 27, 2017 (TBC)
Price: Free
Website: http://www.danijmn.com/flipgrip/

Flip Grip is an addictive, fast-paced arcade runner with original mechanics. Set in a colorful universe, you ride smoothly on vertical walls, tapping to switch sides as you leap over gaps and dodge tricky obstacles. Go as far as you can in survival mode, or try your luck in dozens of carefully handcrafted levels with different laws of physics and mind-bending hurdles.

Features

  • Fast-paced, hardcore tapping gameplay
  • Easy to learn, hard to master vertical wall riding mechanics
  • Endless procedurally generated survival mode
  • 200 meticulously handcrafted challenge levels with varied physics and visuals
  • Leaderboards, achievements and cloud saves
  • Chunky pixels and lots of colors
