Privacy Policy

I, the developer, do NOT collect any information that may personally identify anyone who plays Cardinal Chains or visits this website. I do, however, integrate some widely-used third-party services to provide the optional functionality of achievements and cloud saves. Naturally, those third-party services may have to collect some data from your device to be able to function properly.

The iOS version integrates the third-party service "Game Center", which is provided by Apple. Its privacy policy is available here: https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/gamecenter/

The Android version integrates the third-party service "Google Play Games Services", which is provided by Google. Google's privacy policy is available here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or any other issues, please send an email to support@danijmn.com and I will be glad to help you out.
