Video Policy

You're welcome to create videos of Crossing Chains and upload them to Youtube, Twitch or similar services. Please note, however, that the game contains a licensed soundtrack, so you can either:

  1. Disable the music: open the options menu and set the "Music" slider to 0; or
  2. License the music from Soundstripe: all songs in the game are licensed from Soundstripe. If you make videos for online platforms, chances are you've already heard of this service or may even be using it. If you have an active subscription, you can license the game's songs for free. A playlist of songs featured in Crossing Chains is available for your convenience.
